About Us

Bridging the gap between initial training and the flight line.

A320 Daily was born from a simple observation: technical knowledge decays without active recall. In the busy environment of line operations, it is easy to lose touch with the finer details of the FCOM, FCTM, and Company Operations Manuals.

Our mission is to make professional development effortless. We provide pilots with high-quality, bite-sized learning resources and practical tools that fit seamlessly into their daily routine.
